The Problem

Everyone is moving to AI.
Not everyone got the memo.

800 million people use Windows 10. In 2025, Microsoft ended security support for it. Most of those people have never touched AI — and nobody is coming to teach them. Not Google. Not OpenAI. Not anyone.

They don't have the latest hardware.

Most AI tools require expensive GPUs, fast internet, and cloud subscriptions. Real people have old laptops, slow connections, and no budget for subscriptions they don't understand.

They won't learn it from a YouTube video.

Most AI education is built for people who already know what they're doing. Tutorials assume you know what a terminal is. Documentation assumes you went to college for this. Real people get lost in the first five minutes and never come back.

The Solution

An AI that teaches AI —
step by step, on your machine.

AI-Trainer MAX is a training system that lives on your computer and walks you through AI from the very beginning. No YouTube. No textbooks. No cloud account. Just you, your machine, and a guide that meets you where you are.

1

Download it. Run one file.

Double-click launch-training.bat — that's it. The system handles the rest: health checks, setup, progress tracking.

2

Start at Module 1. Go at your pace.

36 modules across 5 phases. Each one builds on the last. The system tracks where you are and picks up where you left off.

3

Finish with your own personal AI.

By Phase 5, you've built something that belongs to you — a personal AI brain running entirely on your own hardware. No monthly fee. No one else's server. Yours.

The Curriculum

Five phases. Zero assumptions.

Every phase is designed for someone who has never done this before. No prior experience required at any step.

1

Foundation — What AI actually is

Install your first model. Understand what's happening under the hood in plain English. Ask it your first questions.

2

Control — Make it do what you want

Learn how to talk to AI effectively. Stop getting useless answers. Start getting real help.

3

Memory — Give it context about your life

Connect your AI to your own documents, notes, and information so it knows who you are and what you need.

4

Automation — Let it work while you sleep

Build simple workflows that run on their own. AI that does tasks without you having to ask every time.

5

Ownership — Your personal AI brain

A fully personal AI system running on your hardware. Something you built. Something you own. Something you can pass down.
