## KEY TERMS

- **Multiplier**: A person who does not just use a skill — they pass it on. One trained multiplier creates ten more capable people. Ten create a hundred. That is the math that changes communities.

- **Hardening Report**: A document that records the security state of your system — which services are running, what logs say, what needs attention. Defenders write these so the next person knows what to watch.

- **Teaching Draft**: A written guide that takes a complex skill and makes it simple enough for a beginner to follow. Teachers do not just know things — they make others know things too.

- **Brain Export Manifest**: A snapshot of what your AI brain contains — which collections exist, how many entries each holds, when the snapshot was taken. Connectors use these to inventory, transfer, and verify brains.

- **Raw Protocol Call**: Talking directly to the MCP server using curl instead of a wrapper script. Builders understand what happens beneath the tools — the JSON-RPC messages, the HTTP headers, the handshake that makes everything else possible.

### The Four Themes

**DEFENDERS** protect what they built. You locked the gates, spotted threats, backed up and restored data. You know how to scan ports, read security logs, and write hardening reports. When someone's system is exposed, you can find the hole and fix it.

**TEACHERS** pass on what they know. You built workshops, created teaching materials, and learned how to explain local AI to someone who has never seen a terminal. When a neighbor asks "What is AI?", you do not wave your hands. You sit them down and show them.

**CONNECTORS** link systems and people. You exported brains, built family meshes, and learned how to inventory what a brain holds. When two brains need to share knowledge, you know the tools and the formats.

**BUILDERS v2** go deeper. You looked under the hood at the MCP protocol, built prompt chains, and wrote raw API calls. You do not need the training wheels anymore. You can read the spec and build what comes next.

### The Multiplier Effect

Here is the math that matters. You are one person. If you teach one other person everything you learned, there are two of you. If each of you teaches one more, there are four. Then eight. Then sixteen.

That is not a fantasy. That is how every trade, every craft, every real skill has spread through human history. One person teaches another. The apprentice becomes the master. The student becomes the teacher.

800 million Windows users are about to lose security support. Most of them do not know what local AI is. Most of them think AI means paying a company for a subscription and handing over their data. You know better. You proved it. Now you multiply.

### What Happens in This Exercise

The exercise has four sections — one per theme:

1. **DEFENDERS** — You run system_health and security_log_search to build a hardening report
2. **TEACHERS** — You ask the brain a beginner question, generate a teaching guide, and store it in the vault
3. **CONNECTORS** — You inventory your brain with vault_list_categories and search_knowledge to build an export manifest
4. **BUILDERS v2** — You make a raw curl call to the MCP server with no wrapper — just you, the protocol, and the JSON

Eight verification checks confirm you did all four. Pass them all and you earn the Multiplier Certificate.
